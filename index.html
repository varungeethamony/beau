Who it's for

Built for three.
Centred on one.

Every feature in BeAu.ai serves the autistic individual first. Caregivers and therapists are essential — but they orbit the individual, not the other way around.

How it works

Sense. Learn.
Act. Repeat.

The companion runs a continuous loop — reading the individual's state, building its understanding, and responding before distress escalates.

🫀

Sense

The companion reads the individual's state continuously through connected devices.

🧠

Learn

It builds a personal understanding of the individual over time — their patterns, triggers, and what brings them calm.

⚡

Predict

Early signs of distress are detected before a meltdown escalates — while there is still time to act.

🌿

Act

The companion responds with the right support at the right moment — for the individual first.
